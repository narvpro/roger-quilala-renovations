Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026  ·  Applies to roger-quilala-renovations.pages.dev

Roger Quilala Renovations is a sole-proprietor home renovation service based in the Greater Toronto Area, Ontario, Canada. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels. The following limited data interactions occur:

  • Contact form submissions — When you submit the booking form, your browser opens your email app with your details pre-filled. No data is sent to or stored on this website's servers.
  • Google Fonts — Font files are loaded from Google's CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log your IP address as part of that request. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash images — Gallery images are served from Unsplash's CDN (images.unsplash.com). Unsplash may log your IP address. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.
  • Facebook embeds — The embedded Facebook video may load cookies and tracking scripts from Facebook/Meta if you interact with it. See Meta's Privacy Policy.

2. Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Facebook if you interact with the embedded video widget. You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings.

3. How Information Is Used

Any contact details you voluntarily provide via the booking form are used solely to respond to your renovation enquiry. They are not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing without your consent.

4. Data Retention

Because form submissions go directly to Roger's email inbox rather than a database, data retention is governed by standard email practices. You may request deletion of your information by emailing Roger directly.

5. Your Rights (PIPEDA / Ontario)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your information

To exercise these rights, contact Roger directly via the contact form or on Facebook.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out through the booking form on the homepage or message directly on Facebook.
